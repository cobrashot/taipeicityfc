Taipei City FC is Taiwan’s most competitive ex-pat based football team. We are they only ever winners for over a decade of the Taiwan BML taking the crown in both 2008 and 2010. We are also the only ever foreigner team to play in Taiwan’s premier league and are constantly striving to improve the level of football available to the community on the island since our inception in 2007. This season we intend on taking back the BML trophy to its new home at On Tap! The team plays from October to April every year and is made up of students, teachers and business men all with the common goal of playing football at the highest level available to us on this island. The team is represented from 11 different countries this year including England, Ireland, Gambia, Mexico, Nicaragua, Argentina, France and Taiwan to name but a few.
